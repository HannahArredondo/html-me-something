PHOTOGRAPHY IS SOMETHING I DO SOMETIMES

Over the last 6 months, I have learned a lot about photography. I honestly didn't know a thing about photography until I bought a Canon T4i from a friend. That was the motivation I needed to get the ball rolling. Since then I have learned what I enjoy shooting and what isn't my cup of tea. So far I know I enjoy the subjectsanimalsandsports,but I also know I enjoy avariety.


One of my favorite subjects is animals. I have learned to use Shutter Priority Mode since animals move around so much, it is necessary in order to get the subject in focus. A lot of my friends have dogs, so I will take photos for them. I would like to start doing more shoots with people and their dogs though. (Many people there is money in it... and I like dogs.

I have also been learning a lot about sports photography. I have done a few shoots for KC Crew Sports, an intramural sports league here in Kansas City. There was a big learning curve for this, but I finally feel more confident in my ability to shoot sports.

Although I enjoy shooting these specific subjects, I really just enjoy taking photos of random objects, plants, people, walls, buildings, etc. I don't discriminate.
